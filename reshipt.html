Background:

Freshman year of college, I purchased so many things from Amazon and clothing stores to up my style. Often times, however, what I purchased didn't fit or look like the image I had seen online. This meant that I had to return my package at a UPS store, Kohl's, Whole Foods, and other locations that are inconvenient or inaccessible by foot. I did not have a car and did not want to pay $20 round trip for an Uber just to return a package.

This is when I thought of Reshipt...

Design:



Business Model:

Reshipt will operate in a similar way to Uber, Door Dash, Lyft, etc where people may become driver/scontract workers a.k.a. Shipters. Shipters will be paid 50% of each package delivered with the opportunity to recieve a tip. The other 40% would go to corporate to pay employees, R&D, and other expenses to maintain the service.
